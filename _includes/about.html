About ShARE-UP

We are the University of Porto's branch of the international organization ShARE, a student association that gathers the top students, focusing on training them to develop innovative, professional solutions for startups, companies and NGOs.

For the Students

Work on real-world cases, with start-ups, NGOs, high-level companies. Become an exceptional leader and, most of all, learn and share your knowledge

For the Companies

Discover a new way to do consultancy. Tap into a global ecossystem of innovation, fueled by highly trained students that are passionate about learning and helping you grow
