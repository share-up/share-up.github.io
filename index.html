WHAT WE OFFER

As the University of Porto's branch of the international organization ShARE, we connect students companies and NGOs, with the goal of solving real issues. ShARE-UP joins consulting and leadership skills in a think tank culture in order to create "Do Well Do Good" leaders

The Students

Work on real-world cases, with start-ups, NGOs, high-level companies. Become an exceptional Do Well Do Good leader and, most of all, learn and share your knowledge

The Companies

Discover a new way to do consultancy. Tap into a global ecosystem of innovation, fueled by highly trained students that are passionate about learning and helping you grow
